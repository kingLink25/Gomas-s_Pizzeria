Our Team

Our team is made up of the best chefs in the city. We make the best true Italian pizza's.Like so many worthy obsessions, the Goma's Pizzeria story begins in Italy.

Friends Cheese Go and Bread Ma found themselves lured back to Naples time and again, savoring the acclaimed pizza of the region. The duo began to envision something very different: a simple neighborhood pizzeria in the style of those they were inspired by in Naples.

Head Chef Mario, was born in Italy and learnt the craft of making Pizza's from his grandfather when he was just 17 years old. Now he handles around a team of 10 great chef's who churn out the classic Goma Pizzas.

The team launched Goma's Pizzeria first full-service location in Bangalore in 2020.
